Biography

I am a PhD candidate in Anhui Province Key Laboratory of Big Data Analysis and Application, School of Data Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

I am fortunate to be supervised by Prof. Hui Xiong and co-supervised by Prof. Tong Xu. Here is my Google Scholar.

Previously, I received the B.E. degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China in 2019.
