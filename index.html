About me

Zhi Zheng is an postdoc researcher in the State Key Laboratory of Cognitive Intelligence, School of Computer Science and Technology, USTC, under the supervision of Prof. Enhong Chen.

He received his Ph.D. degree in 2024 from School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, USTC, under the supervision of Prof. Hui Xiong and Prof. Tong Xu. Here is his Google Scholar.

From Jun. 2021 to Jul. 2022, he was a research intern in Tencent Jarvis Lab, under the supervision of Dr. Xian Wu and Zhaopeng Qiu.

From Jul. 2022 to Apr. 2024, he was a research intern in Baidu TIC (Jul. 2022 to Dec. 2022) and BOSS Zhipin CSL (Dec. 2022 to Apr. 2024), under the supervision of Dr. Hengshu Zhu.

Previously, he received the B.S. degree in Computer Science from USTC in 2019.

His research interests include Artificial Intelligence and Data Mining, especially Large Language Models and Recommender Systems.
